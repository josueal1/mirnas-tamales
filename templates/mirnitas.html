About Us

Mirnita's is a Salvadoran family business established in 1999. We brought the authentic flavors of La Paz, El Salvador to Los Angeles, CA. Now we are proud to serve our delicious pupusas all over the Greater LA and High Desert area.

Our Story

Our journey started with a passion for sharing the traditional Salvadoran dish, pupusas, with our community. With fresh ingredients and time-honored recipes, we aim to bring a little taste of El Salvador to every plate.
